Frequently asked

How do I log a concert?
Tap +, pick the artist and venue, set the date (tap “Today”), add a star rating and a note.

Is there a free version?
Yes — your first 3 concerts are free. Premium unlocks unlimited entries, photo attachments, and stats.

Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I manage my subscription?
iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
