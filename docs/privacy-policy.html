Privacy Policy — Concert Memory Journal

Last updated: 4 June 2026

Concert Memory Journal ("the app") is designed with your privacy in mind.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Where your data lives

All the information you enter — the concerts you log, ratings, notes, dates, and your premium status — is stored only on your device. None of it is sent to us or to any third party. We have no servers and no account system.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Purchase handling is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Photos

If you attach a photo to a concert (a Premium feature), that photo stays on your device and is never uploaded anywhere by us.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same location with a new "last updated" date.
